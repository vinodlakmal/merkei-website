Enterprise SMS Gateway Optimization

Jasmin SMS Docker SMPP Azure

The Challenge

Our client, a leading financial institution, was facing severe latency and throughput issues with their existing SMS notification system. During peak hours, critical OTPs (One-Time Passwords) and transaction alerts were delayed, leading to poor customer experience and security risks. The legacy system was not scalable and lacked proper failover mechanisms.

Our Solution

Merkei Solutions completely re-architected the delivery infrastructure focusing on high availability and speed:

  • Containerization: Deployed a carrier-grade Jasmin SMS Gateway utilizing Docker for rapid deployment and easy scaling.
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on a highly available Microsoft Azure environment with automated load balancing.
  • Connectivity: Established low-latency SMPP connections directly with major telecom carriers.
  • Monitoring: Integrated 24/7 proactive monitoring to detect and resolve routing issues before they impact delivery.

The Results

The strategic deployment yielded immediate and measurable improvements to the client's operations:

  • 3x Higher Throughput: System now handles thousands of messages per second without bottlenecking.
  • Sub-second Latency: 99% of OTPs are now delivered in under 2 seconds.
  • 99.99% Uptime: Achieved enterprise-grade reliability with automated failovers.
  • Cost Efficiency: Reduced operational and routing costs by optimizing the multicloud structure.
